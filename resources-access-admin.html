Keybox & Access

🔒 LOCKBOX SUPPORT QUICK GUIDE

Use this guide for app access issues, lockbox problems, access hours, and transfers.

Failure of Supra App

If you are experiencing issues with the Supra app or eKEY access:

Call the Supra Support Line directly:
1-877-699-6787

Supra Support is available 24/7 to assist with login issues, access errors, PIN resets, and system troubleshooting.

Failure of Key Boxes

  • Bring the defective Key Box to the Columbus REALTORS® office for a free replacement.

If the lockbox cannot be removed from the property:

Contact Columbus REALTORS®
Monday – Friday, 8:30 A.M. – 5:00 P.M.
614-475-4000
keybox@columbusrealtors.com
(Do not contact a locksmith directly. Columbus REALTORS® will coordinate removal if necessary.)

Transfer of Keyholder to Another Firm

Keyholders using the eKEY and Keybox System must notify Columbus REALTORS® within 14 days of a brokerage affiliation change.

Transfer of Key Boxes

  • Complete a Lockbox Transfer Form
  • Submit within 24 hours of transfer

Contact: 614-475-4000
keybox@columbusrealtors.com • 614-475-4000
